Brand Essence

Who We Are

We deliver fresh, raw foodstuffs directly to your doorstep, we make everyday shopping faster, easier, and more convenient.

Audience

Our audience persona was defined as Sarah, a busy professional balancing work and family life. She values time, convenience, and reliability, preferring the ease of ordering groceries online over the stress of traditional market visits.

Positioning

We position ourselves as the go-to solution for comfort, convenience, and reliability in grocery shopping. Unlike traditional markets, Morrow Food Market eliminates procrastination by bringing the market to you today, not tomorrow.

Personality

Our brand is friendly, approachable, and dependable. We are modern yet warm, with a focus on building trust and community while making life simpler for our customers.

Logo

The Morrow Food Market wordmark was custom-designed with smooth, rounded forms to reflect friendliness and approachability. A unique feature is the two “R”s in the middle of Morrow, which flow seamlessly into each other. Symbolizing the smooth relationship between the brand and its customers. This customization ensures the logo is distinct, memorable, and aligned with the brand's promise of ease and reliability.

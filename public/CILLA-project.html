Brand Essence

Who We Are

Cilla Treats is a cake brand dedicated to delivering joy through beautifully crafted cakes. We combine quality, creativity, and care to make every celebration memorable.

Audience

We serve individuals who value quality cakes, from event planners to brides and everyday celebrants who want something special delivered with warmth and excellence.

Positioning

Cilla Treats stands out by offering more than just cakes, we deliver experiences. With a focus on quality over quantity, we position ourselves as the friendly yet professional choice for life's sweetest moments.

Personality

Our personality is approachable, joyful, and refined. We are warm like a friend, reliable like a professional, and passionate about adding delight to every doorstep.

Logo

For Cilla Treats, I wanted the logo to embody both creativity and warmth. Reflecting the brand's promise of delivering joy. I chose a script-style wordmark to capture the artistry and flow of cake decoration, making it feel handcrafted and personal. To reinforce this idea, the wordmark ends with a piping bag, symbolizing the brand's craft and skill while visually suggesting that the logo itself was “piped” into existence. The color palette was carefully selected to align with the tagline “delivering joy to your doorstep”. It conveys sweetness, delight, and approachability, while still maintaining a professional edge. The overall result is a logo that feels inviting, joyful, and true to the brand's mission of turning every cake into an experience worth remembering.

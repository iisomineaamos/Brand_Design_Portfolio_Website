My Life Is Content Clothing

A Christian apparel brand that empowers Christians to live a life filled with contentment and purpose. Our collections are inspired by Philippians 4:11. We create thought-provoking and heartwarming everyday designs curated as conversation starters, allowing you to share your faith and values with others.

Brand Essence

Who We Are

A yoruba learning platform that connects everyone to their African roots and help them rediscover their identity.

Audience

Our audience persona was defined as Ayo, a 24 year old software engineer seeking to speak fluent yoruba language. She often misses the cultural games she plays in her childhood, she desires a space where she can learn and enjoy her dialect without feeling shy.

Positioning

We identified the unique position Splatter takes is being the only option for this specific format. They add value through the opportunity of freedom and escape they provide as well as the lack of barrier to entry.

Personality

With a strong concept and established strong personality to support it, our archetype was the Outlaw and the primary traits we sought to embody were boldness, creativity, edginess, and fun.

Logo

A clean, minimal mark designed to represent the core values of connection and clarity.

Brand Essence

Who We Are

Steel Cedar Consult is an architecture and interior design firm with a focus on contemporary aesthetics. We deliver premium services to clients who value modern design, high quality, and timeless spaces.

Audience

Our clients are individuals and businesses who appreciate clean, modern design and are willing to invest in high-quality interior and architectural solutions that reflect their lifestyle or brand identity.

Positioning

As a startup, we aim to stand out by offering refined, contemporary design backed by premium service. We position ourselves as a trusted partner in creating spaces that are not only functional but also visually inspiring and memorable.

Personality

Our brand is modern, professional, and sophisticated. We balance creativity with precision, ensuring every detail speaks to quality and every project feels distinctively tailored.

Logo

The idea for this is to make the wordmark the primary identity, this builds recognition and as a start-up we want people to remember our name hence the wordmark would be used for most of our marketing material. The submark is made only from line to form the initials of our brand name SC. This is created as a secondary means of identification in cases where a full brand name won't fit. We can use this logomark stacked with the wordmark to register it on the minds of our target audience (as shown in the previous slide), when we have used this extensively then it can be used as a standalone entity.

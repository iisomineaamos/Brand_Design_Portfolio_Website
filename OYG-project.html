One Yoruba Girl Education

One Yoruba Girl is a Yoruba language learning platform. It was born out of the story of a young, lost girl who did not become until she embraced her authentic Yoruba self and just followed God.

Role

Brand Strategy, Brand Identity, Social Media Design

Year

2023

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Brand Essence

Who We Are

A yoruba learning platform that connects everyone to their African roots and help them rediscover their identity.

Audience

We speak to individuals like Ayo, a 24-year-old software engineer who longs to speak Yoruba confidently and reconnect with childhood cultural experiences. Our audience also includes members of the diaspora seeking an authentic way back to their roots.

Positioning

Unlike typical language apps, One Yoruba sells stories, not just lessons. We position ourselves as the bridge between learning and cultural revival. Where language becomes the pathway to identity and belonging.

Personality

Our brand is warm, confident, and authentic. We blend professionalism with cultural vibrancy, offering a welcoming space where learners can feel proud, connected, and unafraid to embrace who they are.

Logo

The logo features a unity between the O and N of “One” with the O of “Yoruba” shows the essence of the brand in connecting everyone to their African root! The only element with a different color on the logo is the tone mark on the letter "U", which adds a bit of color (fun) that the client wanted. The font is clean and clear which essence it's readability and hence increases brand awareness and recognition. The font tips are made to have straight line edges (like the y, r, and e) which adds to it's professional clean look and feel. The bold font weight makes it stand out and looks confident.

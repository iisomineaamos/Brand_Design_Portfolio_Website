More Than Just a Pretty Picture

Understanding the difference between a logo and a brand is the first step toward building a powerful identity. Here's how I see it, and how I can help.

What is a Logo?

Think of your logo as the face of your business. It’s the visual handshake, the recognizable symbol that people see first. It’s essential, but it’s only one piece of a much larger puzzle.

What is a Brand?

Your brand is the entire experience. It’s the story you tell, the feeling people get, and the promise you deliver. My passion is helping you build that entire brand world, ensuring every piece works together to create something people not only remember, but truly love.

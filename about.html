I'm Iisominea, a brand designer with a passion for blending creativity and science

My mission is simple: to craft strategic, memorable brand identities that turn businesses into brands people recognize, trust, and love.

My Story

I've always been fascinated by the way things work, a curiosity rooted in my love for science. Over time, that same curiosity grew into a passion for design: understanding how colors, shapes, and symbols can influence how people feel and how businesses are remembered. I realized that great design isn't just about making something look beautiful. it's about creating a strategy that helps businesses stand out, connect, and grow with purpose. With every project, I bring a mix of creativity, research, and structure, treating each brand like a living system that deserves to be nurtured and understood. Today, I help passionate entrepreneurs and growing businesses transform their ideas into intentional, memorable brand identities. From logos to full brand systems, I build designs that don't just turn heads, but also earn trust.

At the heart of my work is a simple belief: design backed by strategy creates results that last. And that's the kind of work I'm proud to deliver.

What I Believe In

  • Strategy First: Great design is born from strategy. Every project begins with clarity around your goals and audience. The why before the what.
  • Clarity is Kindness: I believe in clear communication. A simple, intentional design creates the strongest connections. A brand should be easy to understand, easy to remember, and impossible to ignore.
  • Timeless over Trendy: I design brands built to last. Instead of chasing fads, I focus on creating identities that stay relevant, impactful, and trusted for years to come.

Beyond Design

When I'm not crafting brand identities, I'm exploring scientific research in drug discovery. For me, creativity isn't confined to the design world. it's also about solving problems that can change lives. Whether it's shaping a brand or contributing to science, I believe innovation has the power to make a lasting impact.
